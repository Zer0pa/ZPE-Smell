# Public Audit Limits

## What a public audit of this repo can establish
- The repo runs as a standalone smell codec on the committed benchmark surface.
- The committed proof artifact can be reproduced locally.
- The bounded metrics and comparator rows match the committed artifact.
- The repo's non-claims are explicit and testable against the shipped surface.

## What a public audit of this repo cannot establish
- General smell perception.
- Coverage of a full empirical receptor panel.
- Device or product readiness beyond the repo's bounded benchmark surface.
- Admission of this result to the broader certified subset.
