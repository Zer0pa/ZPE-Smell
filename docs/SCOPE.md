# Scope

## Public surface
- One smell codec over a surrogate receptor-response panel.
- One fixed benchmark surface with 35 episodes.
- One bounded result: the codec clears that fixed surface.

## What is in scope
- Single odorants, binary mixtures, ternary mixtures, short adaptation histories, and nuisance cases defined by the committed corpus.
- Retrieval structure, nearest-neighbor recall, mixture separation, fiber separation, adaptation separation, and routing resilience on that corpus.

## What is out of scope
- A full empirical receptor panel.
- General smell perception.
- Device claims, product claims, or deployment claims.
- Any claim that this repo's result is admitted to the broader certified subset.
