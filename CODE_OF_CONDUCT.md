# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
- Be direct.
- Be respectful.
- Keep claims tied to evidence.
- Focus disagreement on code, docs, and measurements.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Misrepresenting benchmark evidence.
- Pressuring maintainers to widen claims beyond what the proof artifacts support.

## Enforcement
Repository maintainers may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this standard.
